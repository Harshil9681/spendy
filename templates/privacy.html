Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 27, 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you use Spendly, we collect information that you provide directly to us. This includes your email address when you create an account, and expense data that you choose to log — such as amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions. We do not collect sensitive financial information like credit card numbers or bank account details.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use the data you provide solely for the purpose of helping you track and understand your spending patterns. Your expense information is used to generate summaries, charts, and reports within your account. We do not use your personal financial data for advertising purposes or share it with third parties for marketing.

3. Data Storage and Security

Your expense data is stored securely on our servers with industry-standard encryption. We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security of your data.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly does not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may use trusted service providers to help us operate our service (such as hosting providers), but these parties are obligated to keep your information confidential and use it only for the services they provide to us.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or delete your personal data at any time through your account settings. If you choose to delete your account, all your expense data will be permanently removed from our servers. You may also export your data for your personal records.

6. Cookies and Analytics

We use minimal cookies to maintain your session while using Spendly. We may collect anonymous usage statistics to improve our service, but this data is not linked to your personal identity or financial information.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically for any changes.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us at support@spendly.example.com.
